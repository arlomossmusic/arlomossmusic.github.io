About Arlo

Based in Calgary, AB, the non-binary singer-songwriter Arlo Moss grew up on mountain air and cold winters. From finding songwriting as an outlet in 2018, they began their journey finding themselves learning multiple instruments and developing their skills over the course of several years spanning most of their teen years and into adulthood, leading to them moving to the U.K. for university at seventeen to study music at the Academy Of Contemporary Music as a vocalist. Now back in their hometown, Arlo is now making indie-folk music and building their career through finding like-minded souls searching for a place where everyone is just a little bit strange. Gathering inspiration from the natural world and human interactions alike, Arlo often draws scenes from their lyrics through subtle observations and quiet details to weave meaningful storylines throughout their songs.

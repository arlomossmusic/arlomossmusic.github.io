simple creature

January 2025

simple creature is a project made to explore the hardships of the human condition and mental health issues that often make life quite difficult for those who suffer from them. Often, mental health is quite solitary and hard to get out of, particularly when alone. This EP uses themes of addiction and isolation to explore the physical and mental impacts of struggling to make it to the next day, hour, even minute. Through these tracks, the hope is to bring even just a fraction of strength back to those who listen, even if it is the strength to cry. At least it's something. This is my something.

2AM Trebles

January 2024

A collaborative project, continuing with the same individuals from prior projects, aiming to create a 2-3 song tracklist inspired from a movie without a previously established musical related to the franchise. This was an amazing opportunity to actively develop collaboration skills in an environment that encourages both personal and professional development. The outcome, a live performance at the Night Owl, Birmingham, was an invigorating, fast-paced experience- and of course, not without a few mishaps- that I am grateful to have been a part of and shared with countless other musicians and students.
